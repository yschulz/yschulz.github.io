Photography

Staging

What I probably enjoy most in photography is to bring a certain idea and inspiration to the silver bromide, or ones and zeros for that fact. Conceptualizing lighting with color, direction and diffusivity in a scene and planning editing decisions confront me with unique challenges, spark my problem solving attitude and can often lead to a learning experience in the process. Besides that, I am a huge fan of storytelling, which I always try to bring aspects of to a photography project through symbolysm, ambience or simple gestures and expressions.

Motorcycles

Being an engineer at heart, it is fair to say that I have an affection to things. This goes from clockworks, intricate movements, complications and contraptions. Exposed assemblies of any kind are just a thing of beauty to me, which is probably a reason why a visit to the hadron collider is still high up on my bucket list. Much more accessible, and therefore my favorite subject in photography, are naked bikes, despite the lack of a license to drive one myself.

Product Photography

When I decided to have a skalpel engraved for the medical doctorate of my sister, I was asked for a photo of the finished piece by the company for their instagram profile. Apparently, this was a very unique request and they would love to display it. Since I was always intrigued by the perfectionism and attention to detail in product photography, I took this opportunity to try myself. With a makeshift light box of a turned over table and white bed linen, I learned the difficulty of capturing shiny metal surfaces the way you want to. Let me tell you, it's tough!

Blog

On this page I want to share everything besides digital art. Things and projects that are off canvas. IRL "art" if you want, but I rather refrain from calling anything on here art. More like projects of artisan nature. I still want to keep things separate from engineering projects and focus on crafts with aspects of aesthetics, maybe cooking related or functional use like furniture. So to whoever this may concern, I hope you enjoy, learn or maybe are inspired from these.

Goldsmithing

A short introduction and my journey into the craft of goldsmithing. From selecting the raw materials and tools to the intricacies of the processes involved, I guide you through my roadblocks and explain how I have overcome them. Pictures of the final products are included of course :)
